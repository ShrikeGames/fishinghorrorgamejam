# Fishing Horror Game Jam 2

I'll be creating a fishing horror game in 14 days. 
Fishing has to be the core mechanic, it must be horror themed, and include a story with some npcs.

Hosted by Figerox Studios
The rules and FAQ about the game jam itself are here: https://itch.io/jam/fishing-horror-jam-2


# Rules

- Fishing must be a main gameplay mechanic, not just a side activity.
- The game must lean into horror.
- Include a story and some NPCs.
- Any engine or tools are allowed.
- Solo or team entries are welcome.
- Reused code and assets are fine, but games must be created during the jam. 

# Optional Theme

- The (completely optional) secret theme for the fishing horror jam is...
- "A Bad Feeling".

# My Idea

-  You're in the middle of a lake fishing
-  You have a hunger system
-  Eat fish or give to one of two npcs
-  Cat - Cat Coins + Shop
-  Dog - Dog Dollars + Shop
-  Fish are corrupted to different degrees (not always visible to start)
-  Characters that eat corrupted fish get more corrupted
-  The deeper you fish the more things become supernatural and eldritch-horrory
-  Cat - Wants large quantities
-  Dog - Wants high quality
-  Fish have corruption, and quality stats (hidden by default)
-  Shop upgrades can improve all aspects of fishing as well as reveal fish stats
-  Each shop has its own currencies
  - Cat Coins
  - Dog Dollars
-  Currencies can only be spent at their respective shops
-  If you buy cosmetics from either shop the opposing npc will comment on them being ugly
-  In general NPC reacts to what you do or do not do


# Dialogue system

  - Generally 2 options per
  - (nice to have) voice acting for the npcs

# Cat Shop

  - Better bait
  - Better hooks to catch more fish at once
  - Cosmetics themed to cats, not fancy
  - Sell cat food (corruption free, cheap, but gives little hunger)
  - Cat App
  - Cat Camera App
  - Corruption Detector

# Dog Shop

  - Strength of line upgrades so the line breaks less
  - Hook upgrades so fish get away less
  - Cosmetics themed to dogs, fancy
  - Sell dog food (corruption free, expensive, but gives decent hunger)
  - Dog app
  - Dog Camera App
  - Quality Detector

# NPC Reactions

React to:
- your clothing
- replacing their app with the other's
- based on how corrupted they are
- how corrupted you are
- If you have completed their requests in a timely manner or not
- the types of fish you sell them
  - Dog wants high quality ones specifically



# Style

Horror games benefit greatly from being in First Person
3D technically
Effectively 2D but give you the first person of a 3D game
Cat/Dog/Fish will be 2D billboards
Tablet on the boat
  - Camera attached to the line looking downward showing the hook and nearby fish
  - Reel-in to go up or release to let the hook sink
  - Spawn fish around the hook just out of sight
  - Functions to direct your boat to either shorelines for the NPCs
  - You can unlock apps on the tablet from the NPCs
    - See how many coins/dollars you have
    - Access shop remotely
    - Video call them?
  - Diagetic UI
    - Show hunger, coins, etc

Food system -> time system
  - Need to eat, it's going up/down all the time
  - Getting to shore takes time (cut out the time with unlockable apps in tablet)
  - Fishing takes time obviously



# Endings
- 1/6 Starve to death
- 2/6 You get corrupted and become an eldritch monster in the lake
- 3/6 Cat gets fully corrupted, dogs get exterminated
- 4/6 Dog gets fully corrupted, dogs enslave all cats and humans
- 5/6 Catch an eldritch horror in the deep (it catches you more like)
- 6/6 Avoid catching the eldritch horror and get the golden fish
  - Return to the world of humans
